Author's notes

This app was written and tested entirely on the android virtual device emulator which was a horrible experience. Many decisions were made poorly as a result such as animation time and swipe angle tolerance. After some testing I settled on a tolerance of 25 degrees for all diagonal angles and 30 degrees for horizontal swipes because it is ergonomically harder to swipe perfectly sideways. I settled on a 1/10 of a second animation and 30fps though one tester complained that it was too fast.

The android SDK was sometimes a joy to use and sometimes are horrible pain. I struggled to understand the meaning of views, contexts, activities and other important objects. Certain things that seemed like they ought to be easy gave me huge trouble. I spent nearly and hour trying fumbling with passing arguments between various project layers.

Using eclipse rather than my usual choice of code editor, Vim, was also a chore for me. Some tools were nice such as suggestions for member variables and methods after typing Class. but navigation and space usage felt very alien to me. I have gained an appreciation for why one would use and IDE like eclipse but currently feel more comfortable and productive in Vim.
